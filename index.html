about us.

Mellou believes that everyone should be able to enjoy the symphony of tastes uncovered in desserts. Our ready-to-eat puddings reimagine taste by blending delight with wellness. We go to great lengths to make sure that our desserts are not only delicious but also vegan, egg-free, gluten-free, and highly adaptable to various dietary restrictions. Whether you love chocolate, fruit, or have an inquisitive palate, a Mellou pudding is ready to sweep your breath away.

about pudding.

Puddings are more than just desserts; they are gastronomic marvels that have captivated palates for decades. Milk, sugar, and a thickening agent-often prepared from gelatin, cornflour, or eggs-are the main ingredients in pudding. Its texture ranges from being silky and smooth to being creamy and custard-like. Puddings can be baked, boiled, or even eaten cold, and each method of preparation results in a different texture and flavour.Accept difference, treat yourself once in a while, and take satisfaction in the unexpected health benefits that a good pudding may offer. So go ahead and enjoy a scoop while the magic of pudding enchants your taste buds.
